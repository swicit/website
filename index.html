SWIC IT Club

Connect with us and explore technology! We meet every Wednesday on campus at 4:30pm in the Information Systems and Business Building Room 2163.

The mission of the SWIC IT Club is to implement an environment where students and alumni with varying levels of technology skills can work together to enhance their academic achievements and experience through volunteer services, activities, and partnerships with local businesses.

This is valuable no matter what profession you are pursuing. You do not need to be going specifically for an IT degree to be a member of SWIC IT Club.
